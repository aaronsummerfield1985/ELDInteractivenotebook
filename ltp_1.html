Lesson Two

Two Listening Skills

Local Listening (The Microscope)

Focus on hearing key points and specific details. The answers are In the passage you hear.

Global Listening (The Telescope)

Ask you to summarize, make predictions or inferences, or give your opinion. Answers are not in the passage.
