Lesson Two

Reviewing the Two Skills

Local Listening (The Microscope)

Focusing on specific, small details like dates, names, or specific numbers. It's like zooming in on one part of a picture.

Global Listening (The Telescope)

Focusing on the big picture, main ideas, and general message. It's like looking at the entire landscape at once.

Lesson Three

Global Listening Keywords

Identify the keywords in each sentence that signal a Global Listening question.

1. What is the main topic of the presentation?
2. What is the passage mostly about?
3. What topic are the students discussing?
4. What is the central idea of the lecture?
5. What are the speaker's feelings about the topic?
